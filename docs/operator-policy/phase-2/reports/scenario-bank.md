# Adsecute Phase 2 Scenario Bank

Decision basis for every scenario: operator authority comes from `decisionAsOf` plus complete-provider-day windows: `recent7d` for trend and veto, `primary30d` for action authority, and `baseline90d` or all-history for memory. The UI-selected reporting range is `analysis_only`; it may explain or contradict but must not authorize action.

Missing, stale, partial, selected-range-only, demo, inaccessible, or contradictory evidence degrades confidence and push eligibility. Do not infer unavailable data from adjacent metrics; mark it `missing`, `derived`, or `requires extension`.
